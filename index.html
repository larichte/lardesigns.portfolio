Meet The Designer

Laura is an aspiring graphic and web designer in the Graphic Information Technology program at Arizona State University. She has had the opportunity to participate in a FBLA graphic design competition in her senior year of high school. Currently, she works at the ASU Print & Imaging Lab as a senior in bindery and apparel.

Outside of school and work, Laura loves to read! She is a huge fan of fantasy books but also enjoys romance and sci-fi. She also loves to travel with family and has visited almost thirty U.S. states.
